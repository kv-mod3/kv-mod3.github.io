About Us

Our Mission & What We Do

Adopt a Bread is an international non-profit organization which uses the power of social media and the internet to educate consumers about the benefits of adopting or sharing a loaf, while creating lasting connections between bakeries across the globe. The importance of adopting bread has been recognized by various food organizations such as The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), Slow Food USA, and others. Every year, millions of bread lives are saved through our efforts in spreading awareness. We use online platforms such as Facebook, Pinterest, Twitter, Instagram, etc., and other more traditional forms of outreach such as printed materials and newspaper articles. Adoption rates range from 5% to over 50%, depending on location and bakery's adoption policy. Our goal is to raise awareness and increase adoption among the general population to 10%. The more we can reach new adopters, the higher percentage rate of success becomes. We've raised hundreds of thousands of dollars for the preservation and rescue of many different breads such as:

  • Ciabatta
  • Oatmeal Raisin Sandwich Loaf
  • Honey Buns
  • French Country Bread
  • Rye Sourdough
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Baguette Bread
  • Pizza Dough

Additionally, we also save and rescue gluten-free breads, dairy-free breads, vegetarian breads, nut breads, fruit breads and other unique varieties of breads. We believe that every human should adopt a loaf of bread each month to help ensure that the life of the bread is preserved in their mouth. A happy loaf is a bread that knows its purpose and remains full of flavor even after it's been eaten.
